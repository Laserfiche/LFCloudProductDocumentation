July 2022 Updates

The July 2022 update, released on July 7, 2022, includes the following new features.

Watch an overview of the new features here:

Access Control for Starting Business Processes

Control who can launch specific business processes. New privacy settings allow configuring access at the user, group, or team level.

Business Process Email Notification Updates

Business Processes have new email notifications for reminders and alerts.

You can now add reminders to user tasks to send scheduled emails to the user the task is assigned to. Administrators can resend these reminder emails directly from the Monitor page when viewing the instance’s history or tasks.

A new option in Process Properties allows for email notifications when a process is suspended as well as terminated.

Saved Form Page Orientation

Form Settings now include an option where you can choose between portrait and landscape orientation when printing, downloading, and saving a form. The option does not affect the form's appearance in a web browser.

New Solution Templates

The Solution Marketplace has new solutions including templates for incident reporting and incoming raw material inspections.
