January 2023 Updates

The January 2023 update, released on January 5, 2023, includes the following new features.

Watch an overview of the new features here:

Project Security Migration

To take advantage of the additional security and classifications available with project security, you can now migrate existing forms and business processes from the Global project to a specific project. You can also migrate forms and business processes from a specific project to Global.

New Solution Templates

New solution templates include a new patient intake process that provides an option for patients to fill out patient information prior to their office visit.
