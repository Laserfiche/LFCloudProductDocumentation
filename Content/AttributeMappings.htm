Attribute Mappings in &

Follow the steps below to map attributes between and .

Attribute Mappings in

  1. Sign in to .
  2. Click the Applications tab and click your application. To learn more about creating an application, see Create a Application in .
  3. Click the General tab.
  4. In the SAML Settings section, click Edit.
    • Click Next to navigate to the Configure SAML section.
    • Scroll down to the Attribute Statements section.
    • Enter the Name of the attribute. Leave Name format as Unspecified. Enter a Value for the attribute.

      Note: To view a complete list of attributes, click the Assignments tab. Click the name of a person in the People tab. Click the Profile tab. To learn more, navigate to 's help documentation.

    • Click Add Another to add more attribute statements.
    • When finished, scroll down and click Next. Then click Finish.

Attribute Mappings in

  1. Navigate to and sign in.
  2. Click the Settings tab under .
  3. Click Single Sign-On tab. If you have not configured with your identity provider, see Configuring SAML Single Sign-On with .
  4. Click the Attribute Mappings tab.
  5. Enter the attribute Name that you specified in . Do not use the attribute Value.
  6. Once finished, click Save changes.
