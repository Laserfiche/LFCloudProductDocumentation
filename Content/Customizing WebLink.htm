Customizing WebLinkPublic Portal

WebLink Public Portal can be customized to meet your organization's needs. For example, you can add custom links to your Welcome page, modify search options, and alter the default settings of your WebLink installation, change the site's colors and logos.

There are two starting locations for customizing WebLink. The WebLink Designer provides a graphical front end for modifying many of the WebLink components. If you are comfortable with directly modifying code, the WebLink installation includes the Visual Studio 2008 project. See the following sections for more information.

  • Customizing WebLink through the WebLink Designer
  • Customizing WebLink through Visual Studio

To customize Public Portal, you can use the Public Portal Designer. User accounts with the Public Portal Administrator right can access the Designer from the app picker when signed in to Laserfiche Cloud.

To view the newly customized pubic portal, click the username at the top right, then click View Public Portal.
