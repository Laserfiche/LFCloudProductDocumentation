Requesting a Signature on a Document

You can send documents in to to be signed. will export the selected documents to . Designated recipients will receive an email message notifying them that documents are awaiting their signature. If you have configured to store information from in the document, that information will be updated once the document has been signed, declined, voided, sent, or delivered; see Configuring Settings for more information.

Enabling the Integration

Before you can request a signature, you must associate your user account with a account. To do so, log in to and go to the repository. In the upper left corner, click your user name and select Options. Select Advanced, and then provide your email address and password in the Account section. Click Validate to validate your credentials.

Requesting a DocuSign Signature

  1. Select one or more documents in the Folder Browser.
  2. Click the More Actions () button, then click Request Signature with .
  3. In the Documents section, you will see a list of the documents that you have selected to send to . Click + Add Document to append additional documents to the envelope. When you have multiple documents, reorder documents by dragging and dropping the icon.
  4. To remove documents from the envelope, hover over a document and click the red x.
  5. In the Template drop-down list, select the appropriate template. templates define signing instructions for specific sets of documents.

    Note: Make sure that the number of selected documents and document order is compatible with the desired template.

  6. The Recipients section will contain one or more rows. Specify the name and -mail address of each person from whom you want to request a signature. Laserfiche can access your Address book to facilitate auto-completion of names and e-mail addresses.

    7. Note: Depending on the selected template, some recipients can be left blank. Required recipients are marked and must be filled in before sending to .

  7. In the Message tab, customize the contents of the email notification. A default subject and message may be associated with the template.
  8. Click Request Signature to send the selected documents to . Designated recipients will receive an email message from asking them to review and sign the submitted documents.
