Enable Single Sign-On for your App

After you finish adding a application to , follow the steps below to set up single sign-on (): 

  1. In the portal, select Enterprise applications.
  2. Find and select the App.
  3. Select to open the configuration page.
  4. On the Configuration page, you may choose between one of the options below:
    • Option A: Manually fill in the required fields:
      • In the Basic Configuration section, click the Edit button.
      • Navigate to Account Administration in .
        • Click the Settings tab.
        • Click the Single Sign-On tab.
        • Click Service Provider Information. Keep the dialog box open to use the information to finish filling out the required fields in Azure.

          • Note: If this is your first time configuring single sign-on in , please see Configuring for Single Sign-On ().

      • Navigate back to and finish filling out all required fields.
    • Option B: Upload metadata file:
      • Navigate to Account Administration in .
        • Click the Settings tab.
        • Click the Single Sign-On tab.
        • Click Service Provider Information.
        • Click download Laserfiche metadata file.
      • Navigate back to and click Upload metadata file button.
      • Click Select a file.
      • Navigate to the location the metadata file was downloaded on your computer.
      • Click Add to finish.

    5. Note: To learn more about next steps, see Configuring Single Sign-On with .
