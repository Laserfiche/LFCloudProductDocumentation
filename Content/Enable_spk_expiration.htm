Service Principal Key Expiration

Once service principal keys are created, the expiration date cannot be modified. You must modify the number of days until expiration before creating the keys.

To modify the service principal key expiration date:

  1. Click Settings.
  2. Click Integration Configuration.
  3. Click Service Principal Keys.
  4. Ensure Enable service principal key expiration is selected and enter the number of days until expiration. You can also clear this option to disable key expiration.
  5. Click Save changes.
