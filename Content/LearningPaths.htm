Learning Paths

Use the learning paths below to familiarize yourself with Laserfiche, depending on your role and needs. You will not need to review each resource listed in its entirety. Rather, resources are provided as references to help you achieve each learning milestone. Many of the resources are hosted in Laserfiche Aspire which provides hundreds of free tutorial videos, eLearning courses, getting started guides, and more. Learn more about Laserfiche Aspire and how to create an account.
