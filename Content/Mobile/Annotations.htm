Annotations

You can view annotations on a document and see information about when the annotations were configured and by whom.

To view annotations

  1. Open a document.
  2. Tap the annotation button on the top toolbar.
  3. Optional: Tap Show to see the annotations on an imaged document.

    4. Note: Once you show annotations on one document, they will be shown on all documents. Tap Hide to no longer display annotations.

  4. All the annotations on the document are listed in the order in which they appear on the document. Included in the list is the user who added the annotation, the time or date it was added, the annotation type, and any text in the annotation (e.g., the contents of a sticky note).
  5. Tap the close button to close the annotation list.
