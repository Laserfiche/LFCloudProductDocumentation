Browsing

When you log into Laserfiche Mobile, you will see your documents and folders. Tap a folder to access its contents, or tap a document to open it in the Document Viewer.

Note: You can return to your Folder Browser at any time by swiping right and tapping All Files.

  • Back: To navigate up one level (to the parent folder of the folder you are in), tap the back button in the top left on an Apple device, or tap the back button on an Android device.
  • Refresh: To refresh the contents of the current folder, tap the refresh button in the top right.
  • Search: To search for documents or folders, tap the search button in the top right of an Android device, or pull down to reveal a search box on an Apple device.
  • New folder or document: To add a new folder or document, tap the Add button in the top right. Learn more.
  • Fields and more: You can interact with a document or folder using a variety of options. To access these options, long press the document or folder on an Android device or swipe left on a document or folder on an Apple device. To interact with multiple documents and folders at once, select the check box next to them and tap the More button in the top right.
  • Main Menu: To navigate between libraries, recent uploads, help, and app information, as well as to log out of the app, swipe right or tap the main menu button to go back to the main menu.
