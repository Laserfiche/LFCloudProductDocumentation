Deleting Documents and Folders

To delete one document or folder

  • If using an Android device, long press the document or folder. If using an Apple device, swipe left on the document or folder. Tap Delete. To confirm the deletion, tap OK in the Confirm dialog box.

To delete multiple documents and folders

  1. Tap the checkbox next to one or more documents or folders.
  2. Tap the More button in the top right, then tap Delete.
  3. To confirm the deletion, tap OK in the Confirm dialog box.
