Finding Documents and Folders

There are four different ways to find documents in Laserfiche Mobile:

  • Browsing: Navigate your folder system.
  • Libraries: Use a personalized library to store documents you want quick access to.
  • Searching: Search based on document text, entry name, field values, and annotation text.
  • Recent Uploads: Access documents or folders that were added to Laserfiche in the last seven days. You can pause the uploading process from here as well.
