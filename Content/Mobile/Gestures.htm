Gestures

Laserfiche Mobile supports the following gestures:

Tap: With one finger, touch the screen briefly.

 

Long press (Android only): With one finger, press the screen and wait.

 

Double tap with one finger: With one finger, tap the screen twice. The second tap should closely follow the first.

 

Swipe: Touch the screen and drag your finger quickly in one direction.

 

Scroll: Touch the screen with one finger and drag or swipe away from the direction you want to scroll (e.g., if you want to scroll up, drag your finger down).

 

Pinch: Touch the screen with two fingers and drag them closer to each other.

 

Spread: Touch the screen with two fingers and drag them away from each other.

 

Split Keyboard (iPad only): When the keyboard is showing, touch and hold both thumbs on the middle of the keyboard and drag them away from each other.

 

Join Keyboard (iPad only): When the keyboard is present and split, touch and hold both thumbs on the keyboard and drag them toward each other.
