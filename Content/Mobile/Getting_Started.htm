Working with documents and folders

There are a variety of options for interacting with documents and folders. To access these options, long press the document or folder on an Android device or swipe left on the document or folder on an Apple device. To interact with multiple documents and folders at one time, select the checkboxes next to them and tap the More button in the top right.
