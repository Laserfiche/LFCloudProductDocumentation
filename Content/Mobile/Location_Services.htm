Location Services

Location Services allows location-dependent apps and web sites to use information from cellular, Wi-Fi, and Global Positioning Systems (GPS) to determine your approximate location.

Laserfiche Mobile supports geotag fields which are fields that record the location a photo was captured. For the app to record the location a photo is captured, the device using Laserfiche Mobile must have Location Services enabled.

To turn on Location Services for Laserfiche Mobile on an Apple Device

  1. Tap Settings from the Home screen.
  2. Tap Privacy, then Location Services.
  3. Slide the toggle next to the Laserfiche app to ON.

To turn on Location Services for Laserfiche Mobile on an Android Device

  1. Open your device's Settings.
  2. Tap Location services.
  3. Ensure the relevant location services are enabled.

Note: The specific location services options will vary by device.
