Move and copy documents and folders

You can move and copy documents and folders with Laserfiche Mobile.

Move or copy one document or folder

  1. If using an Android device, long press the document or folder. If using an Apple device, swipe left on the document or folder. Tap Move To or Copy To.
  2. Navigate to where you want to move or copy the document or folder and tap Move here or Copy here.

Move multiple documents or folders

  1. Tap the checkbox next to one or more documents or folders.
  2. Tap the More button in the top right.
  3. Tap Move To.
  4. Navigate to where you want to move the document or folder and tap Move here. To cancel the move, tap Cancel.

Copy multiple documents or folders

  1. Tap the checkbox next to one or more documents or folders.
  2. Tap the More button in the top right.
  3. Tap Copy To.
  4. Navigate to where you want to copy the documents or folders and tap Copy here. To cancel the copy, tap Cancel.

Create a new folder while moving or copying documents or folders

  1. Tap Move To or Copy To as described in the instructions above.
  2. Tap the new folder button (Apple) (Android) in the top right.
  3. Enter a name for the new folder and tap OK to save your changes. To cancel the creation of the new folder, tap Cancel.
  4. Continue copying or moving the entry as desired.
