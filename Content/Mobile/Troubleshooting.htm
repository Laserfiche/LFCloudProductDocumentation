Troubleshooting

Connection

If you have trouble connecting to Laserfiche, contact your administrator.

Viewing Documents Edited Outside Laserfiche Mobile

If a document or folder has been edited outside of Laserfiche, you will need to refresh the document or folder. Navigate to the Folder Browser and tap refresh on the top toolbar. If a template or field has been changed, it will be updated when you reopen the Fields page.

Restarting the app

If the app freezes or is behaving unexpectedly, you may need to restart it.

Android

  1. Navigate to your device's Settings menu.
  2. Tap Apps (or Applications).
  3. Tap Laserfiche in the list of apps. Depending on your device you may need to navigate through a Manage Apps or Downloaded menu, etc.
  4. Tap Force stop.
  5. Press the Home button to go back to the Home screen and restart the app.

Apple

Press the Home button twice and swipe through the apps until you see the Laserfiche Mobile app.

  • In iOS 6, hold your finger on the app until you see a red minus icon appear. Tap the red minus icon to close the app.
  • In iOS 7, swipe the image above the Laserfiche icon up and off the screen.

Press the Home button to go back to the Home screen and restart the app.
