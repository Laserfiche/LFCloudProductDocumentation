Working with Documents and Folders

You can interact with documents and folders while inside the Folder Browser. If using an Android device, long press a document or folder to see a variety of options. If using an Apple device, swipe left on a document or folder to see a variety of options. To interact with multiple documents and folders at one time, select the checkboxes next to the them and tap the More button in the top right.
