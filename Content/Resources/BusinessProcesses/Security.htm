Security Roles for Business Processes

Security roles determine users' access and rights to business processes and forms. To prevent users from making unauthorized changes, you should assign your users security roles that fit their organizational responsibilities.

The security role assigned to a user determines their permissions with respect to individual forms, business processes (including the forms in them), and teams.

Security for Business Processes

To learn the permissions that each role has with respect to business processes, read about security roles in Process Automation. The rights that each role has for business processes are exactly the same as the rights that each role has for other processes (e.g. workflows) in Process Automation.

Security for Independent Forms

In addition to the standard Process Automation security roles, there is an additional Form Developer role, which allows users to create surveys for independent use outside of business processes.

For individual forms in a process, the security roles in Process Automation correspond to the following privileges.

Process Administrator

  • Full administrative access to business processes and teams

Process Asset Administrator

  • Can

Process Developer

  • Full administrative access to business processes and teams

Process Manager

  • Read-only access to business processes and surveys. Process managers can run a business process, start a survey, or update survey details like name and description, but cannot publish surveys or business processes.
  • Limited access to teams. Process managers can view, modify, and create teams, but cannot delete or export teams except for teams they have created themselves.

Form Developer

  • Can create surveys.
  • Has full administrative rights over all forms

Team Administration

When a user has full administrative access to a team, that means they can create, modify, view, export, and delete teams. They will also see all teams in the drop-down menu of teams under Team Tasks, and can view tasks from all teams in the Tasks tab.
