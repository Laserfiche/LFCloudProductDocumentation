Custom Reports

Custom reports let you create custom data visualizations, including line charts, pie charts, bar charts, and more from your business process data. These reports enable organizations to gain deeper insights into processes to make more informed decisions.

Select Custom Reports from the process automation home page to create, view, and manage your custom reports.
