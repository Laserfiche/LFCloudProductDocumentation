Managing Custom Reports

From the main Custom Reports screen, you can create new reports, see the list of existing reports, modify report properties, and delete reports.

Show Me:

 

To create a new report, click the New button.

To search for a report by name, click the search bar, type part of the name of the report, and click the magnifying glass icon.

The left panel lets you choose whether the list displays current or deleted reports.

The center panel displays a list of the reports, and provides information about the reports. The immediately visible elements are the report name, the process the report is based on, and the style of the report (type). Additional items include creation and modification details.

The right panel will display detailed report information for a selected report. You can change the name and description of a selected report within this panel as well.

To select a report, click on the row for the report.

To open a report, click on the linked report name in the row, or select the report and click the pencil icon at the top right corner of the table .

To delete a report, select the report and click the trash can icon at the top right corner of the table.
