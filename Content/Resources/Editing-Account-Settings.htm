Editing Account and Site Settings

This page describes how to change your account and site settings. The account settings lets you view your name, email, and user name, and change your password. To change your name, email, and user name, go to the app picker on the top right, select Account and then Users, and then select the account. The site settings lets you change the language and time zone on the site.

To change your account settings:

  1. Click your name in the top right of the header.
