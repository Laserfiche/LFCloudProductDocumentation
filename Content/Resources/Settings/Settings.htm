Settings

With Settings, you can manage various aspects about your organization. The resources you set up here can be used throughout your workflows. Currently, Settings lets you create the following types of resources:

  • Work Schedules: Select the weekends (non-business days) and holidays excluded in your workflows.
  • Holidays: Specify the holidays counted in the Exclude weekends and holidays option.
  • Email Display Names: Create sender display names for email messages sent as part of a business process.
