Copying, Cutting, and Pasting Activities

The copy, paste, and cut options in the workflow designer toolbar, along with the copy, paste, and cut keyboard keys (CTRL + c, CTRL + v, and CTRL + x), let you copy or cut activities and their configuration details from one workflow and paste them onto another workflow.

You can copy, paste, and cut activities when you are using:

  • Many workflow tabs within one browser tab.
  • Many browser tabs within one browser window.
  • Many browser windows within one browser (e.g., all within Google Chrome or within Mozilla Firefox).

You cannot:

  • Copy, paste, and cut across different browsers (e.g., from Google Chrome to Mozilla Firefox).
  • Copy or cut an activity, log out, log in, and paste the activity.
  • Drag and drop activities from one canvas onto another.
