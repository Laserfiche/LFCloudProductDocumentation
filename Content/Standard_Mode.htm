Scanning in Standard Mode

Standard mode is one of two Laserfiche Scanning modes. It provides a more powerful interface that offers more processing options and allows you to scan multiple documents to different locations in a single session.

Standard scanning mode offers:

  • A wider range of image enhancements and processes, in addition to those available in basic mode.
  • The ability to scan multiple documents at once and save them to different locations.
  • Dynamic storage options.
  • A wider variety of default document properties.
