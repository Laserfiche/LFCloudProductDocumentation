Video Resources

The following videos cover a wide variety of topics from working with documents to automating your processes.

Video Features

All videos have the following features:

  • Closed captioning: Click the closed captioning button [CC] to see subtitles for the video.
  • Link to help pages: Near the end of most videos, a callout will appear telling you to check out the documentation for more information. Click this callout to go directly to the documentation while you're watching the video.
  • Search: Click the list button to search for a specific term in the video's script. Click any search result to jump to that section of the video.
  • Playback speed: Click the gear icon to change the speed at which the video plays.
  • Full screen mode: Click the pop out button to open the video in a new tab. In this tab, click the full screen button to open the video in full screen mode.
