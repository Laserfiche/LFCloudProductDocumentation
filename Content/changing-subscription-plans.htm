Subscription Plan Types

offers four new package tiers:

  • Starter

    • Positioned for small departments or small businesses with paperless initiatives that want to quickly get started with a best-in-class document management system to securely capture, organize and centralize their digital documents. It includes user access controls to ensure sensitive information is only accessible to the right team members.

  • Professional

    • Ideal for small- and medium-sized business requiring process automation and document management functionalities to transform entire processes from individual teams to whole departments. It provides powerful forms and process designers, reporting and analytics, basic audit trail capabilities, scripting and integration tools, full-featured document management and more to support any departmental automation initiative.

  • Business

    • Tailored for large businesses that need advanced administration tools to support large-scale deployments across multiple departments and those who are in regulated industries and must support compliance policies. Also includes the ability to share content with and receive information from clients and the public through customer portals.

  • Enterprise

    • Relevant for the largest and most sophisticated enterprises that require maximum flexibility and support to scale solutions across geolocations with access to complete functionality to incorporate platform as part of their enterprise technology stack.
