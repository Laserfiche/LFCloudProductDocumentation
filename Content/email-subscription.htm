Subscribing to Email Notifications

can send email notifications when an account approaches storage and transfer limits. On the Plan page, authorized users with a verified email address can click Subscribe to receive alerts when storage limits meets the 75%, 90%, 95%, and 100% thresholds.

Solution Providers

Solution Providers can also subscribe to alerts for customer accounts. On the customer listing page, click Manage Email Subscriptions to subscribe to multiple customer accounts at once.

  1. In the Not subscribed to section, select the accounts you want to receive usage alerts. Then click Add to move the selected accounts to the Subscribed to list.
  2. If you no longer wish to receive alerts for an account, select the account in the Subscribed to list and click Remove.
  3. Click Save Changes to complete the subscription.
